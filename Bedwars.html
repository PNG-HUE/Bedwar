About Bedwars

Bedwars is a popular game mode in Minecraft where players team up to protect their bed while attempting to destroy the beds of other teams.

Strategies

Discover the best strategies to win in Bedwars!

  • Protect your bed with layers of different materials.
  • Gather resources quickly and upgrade your gear.
  • Communicate with your team effectively.
  • Be aggressive but also defend your base.
